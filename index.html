Moosic Discord Bot
This project was created with a goal in mind, a solution to the ban-wave of all renowned discord music bots due to copyright issues and a form of entertainment for my friends and I.

The development of this project requires me to grasp the basics of DiscordJS. This was all done with tutorial videos published online. The creation of the discord bot requires the integration of a public library of an exhaustive lists of songs so as to avoid the copyright issues implied by YouTube on discord bots.

This was not a personal creation (this was built off another developers' codes). However, I was behind the creation of the entire bot from commands and message replies by the bot to library integration to connection with discord via the developers' portal and lastly to hosting of the bot online on Replit.

Completing this project, my friends and myself are able to enjoy music on discord for our entertainment and a sense of pride and ownership into a discord bot that can showcase my abilities to learn outside of my academic scope.

EventNow - Event Management Platform
As students, my teammates and I agree that it is frustrating to look for volunteering or upskilling workshop opportunities due to the lack of attention and resources dedicated to building a proper platform. Henceforth, we decided to tackle this issue as one of our school projects, to combine all school and external events and opportunities together on one, centralized, platform.

This was a project development that my team focused on the usage of C#, MySQL, and APIs.

As a member in the development of this project, I focused on the main page where all events are displayed. This requires me to focus on utilising ASP.NET to create a user interface that is appealing. Additionally, C# itself to connect the backend processes like MySQL database to retrieve and display all the information on the webpage.

Moreover, I also worked on the creation of accounts and saving of all relavant information such as sign-ups to each account on a database.

The final product of this project was a website that successfully meet our original goals of the project and the dynamic and responsive features that flawlessly updates (in real time) all and any information to the database.
